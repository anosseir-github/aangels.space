About

A & Angels Space Propulsion is located in South-Holland few steps from the TU Delft with HQ in Den Haag.

The company designs and develops SOTA-competent Green Propulsion for small and medium-sized spacecraft as well as Kick-stages; guided by Industry 4.0 (4IR) and aiming at green sustainable industrialization for Space 4.0 era.
